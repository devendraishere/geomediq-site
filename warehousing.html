What we do

Storage and distribution, treated as part of the product.

For temperature-sensitive pharma and nutraceutical goods, warehousing isn't a back-office step — it's where shelf-life and compliance are protected. We're building the facility to store your stock under the right conditions, track every batch and expiry, and dispatch it correctly — so what leaves us is exactly what your customer should receive.

Services

Storage plus fulfilment — under one roof.

01 · STORE

Temperature-controlled storage

Cold-chain (2–8°C), controlled room temperature and ambient zones — with monitoring, backup power and validated conditions for sensitive stock.

  • Continuous temperature monitoring & alerts
  • Backup power for cold-chain continuity
  • Palletised, secured, segregated storage
02 · TRACK

Batch & expiry control

Every consignment logged by batch and expiry, picked on a first-expiry-first-out basis so near-dated stock never sits behind fresh inventory.

  • Batch & lot-level inventory records
  • FEFO (first-expiry-first-out) discipline
  • Near-expiry flagging & reporting
03 · FULFIL

Picking, packing & dispatch

Orders received, picked, packed and dispatched accurately — so your distributors and customers get the right stock, in the right condition, on time.

  • Order picking & packing
  • Condition-appropriate outbound packaging
  • Documentation & dispatch coordination
04 · DISTRIBUTE

Distribution reach

From our Jaipur hub across Rajasthan and pan-India — with cold-chain integrity maintained from our shelf to the next link in your chain.

  • Rajasthan & pan-India distribution
  • Cold-chain-aware outbound logistics
  • Coordinated with your existing carriers

How it works

From inbound pallet to dispatched order.

Inbound & check

Goods received, inspected and logged by batch and expiry.

Store

Placed in the correct temperature zone — cold-chain or ambient.

Monitor

Conditions tracked continuously; deviations flagged early.

Pick & pack

Orders fulfilled FEFO and packed for safe transit.

Dispatch

Documented and sent out across Rajasthan and India.
