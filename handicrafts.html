How it works

Sourcing, made simple.

From your first inquiry to goods at your door — four clean stages. No haggling theatre, no missing weeks, no surprise costs.

Tell us your story

Send your store, your aesthetic and your target retail price. We curate from our catalogue and reply with what fits — not what we want to push.

↳ Reply within 2 hours

Sample & quote

Pick samples; we dispatch by courier in 5 working days. You get a transparent, line-item quote with freight and duties to your door.

↳ Quote in 24 hours

Production & updates

Order placed on a 30% advance. We split production across partner karkhanas and send photo & video updates twice a week.

↳ 30–45 day lead time

QC, ship, follow up

↳ Door delivery, 7–12 days
